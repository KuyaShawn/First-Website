Transmission Repairs

Reliable Transmission Shop & Repair specializes in transmission repairs and currently been doing it ever since we have started and as we know, your transmission is a vital part of your car as it helps it moving your car. By time and each usage, your car's transmission will someday retire, this is where we come to help as plenty of people go through this process.

In our Transmission checkups we usually clean out your transmission to see if any leakages occur when the car is on and when the wheels are spinning, this usually helps us spot the problem right away as it helps pinpoint on which component needed to be opened. Another thing we check is for cracks or holes in your transmission, this is usually caused by overwhelming the transmission or driving a car with no transmission fluid left, this type of damage is usually the worst ones. As you would probably need a new transmission. Lastly, we check for the fluid in your transmission if it is low or if the fluid is very dirty. With these checkups, you will have a healthy running transmission for a while.
